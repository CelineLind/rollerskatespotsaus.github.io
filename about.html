About

Rollerskate Spots Australia was created out of a need for finding rollerskate (and rollerblade) friendly skate spots! Finding those sweet level-ground, smooth surface places in our neighbourhoods can be a struggle, especially if you don't know where to look.

Acknowledgement of Traditional Owners

We acknowledge the Traditional Owners of the land on which we skate and pay respect to Elders past and present. This website was built on Yuggera and Waga Waga land.
