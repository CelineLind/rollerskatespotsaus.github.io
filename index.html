Submit a Skate Spot!

Fill out the Google Form linked below to add a Skate Spot! The more detail and pictures you can provide the better!

Skate spots will be listed on the site as unverified until we have more than one person vouch that the location is what it claims to be. Exclusions to this rule are skate spots with information already available online, or if images submitted provide signs or other identifiable landmarks in them.
