Rollerdrome

Address: 19 Caloundra Road, Caloundra West

City: Caloundra, Sunshine Coast // Gubbi Gubbi

Information

This roller rink located in downtown Caloundra is air conditioned and has skate hire available. There is also a cafe and toilets inside and lots of parking.
