Skateaway

Address: 700 Albany Creek Rd, Albany Creek QLD 4035

City: Albany Creek, Brisbane // Yuggara

Information

This roller rink located on the northside of Brisbane hosts many skate sessions throughout the week. The rink is air conditioned and has skate hire available. There is also an internal cafe, toilets and is located directly above a small shopping centre. This roller rink also sells skates.
