Fraser Park

Address: Golden Beach QLD 4551

City: Caloundra, Sunshine Coast // Gubbi Gubbi

Information

Fraser Park hosts a kids playground and has a concrete biking path running beside it. This path forms part of the Sunshine Coast 'Coastal Pathway'. To the north this path runs parallel to the ocean with some, but not too many, trees that may drop small sticks. To the south it looks like thicker patches of trees that may drop more debris. The ground is mostly level with some slight ups and downs that could startle an unaware beginner.

Verified: No

Slope: Mostly Flat

Surface: Smooth Concrete

Cracks: Small

Difficulty: Beginner
