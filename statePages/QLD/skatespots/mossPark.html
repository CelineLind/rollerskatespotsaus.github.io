Moss Park

Address: Moss Park, Compton Rd, Slacks Creek QLD 4127

City: Slacks Creek, Brisbane // Yuggera

Information

A short 1-2km path going from Compton Rd to Moss St. There is also an entry on Allgas St which is quite sloped and not recommended for beginners.

Verified: Yes

Slope: Mostly Flat

Surface: Smooth Bikeway Concrete

Cracks: Small

Difficulty: Beginner
