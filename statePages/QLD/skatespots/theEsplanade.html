The Esplanade

Address: The Esplanade, Caloundra QLD 4551

City: Caloundra, Sunshine Coast // Gubbi Gubbi

Information

The Esplanade forms part of the Sunshine Coasts 'Coastal Pathway'. It isn't the friendliest section for skaters, with stretches of boardwalks to maneuver. The concrete patches are doable with average sized cracks that you could step over. A lovely area, but not recommended for beginners.

Verified: Yes

Slope: Mostly Flat

Surface: Rough Concrete, Boardwalks

Cracks: Medium

Difficulty: Intermediate
